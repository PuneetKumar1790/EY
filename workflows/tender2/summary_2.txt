**6. Technical Specifications (Core Product Data)**
This section provides a detailed breakdown of the technical requirements for each product, which is paramount for accurate product matching and compliance checks. Listing all parameters for each product variation allows for precise SKU mapping and ensures that Asian Wires can meet the exact specifications. This data forms the basis for technical evaluation and quality assurance.

The tender specifies XLPE insulated Aluminum Aerial Bunched Cables with a rated voltage of 1100 volts, confirming to IS-14255/1995 for ABC cables, IS-8130/1984 for conductors, IS-398/Pt.IV/1994 for aluminum alloy conductors, and IS-10418/1982 for cable drums. The XLPE insulation must conform to IS-7098(Part-II):85 and be black in color. Phase conductors are identified by one, two, or three ridges. The conductors are required to be compacted.

**Technical Specifications for LT AB Cable, 1C x 35mm² + 1C x 16mm² + 25mm² messenger:**
*   **Cable Type:** LT Aerial Bunched Cable, XLPE insulated Aluminum.
*   **Core Configuration:** 1 Phase Conductor, 1 Neutral Conductor, 1 Messenger Wire.
*   **Phase Conductor:** 35 mm², Aluminum, 7 strands, 6.8 mm compacted diameter, approx. 95 Kg/KMs mass, Max. DC Resistance at 20ºC of 0.868 Ohm/km. Tensile strength of aluminum wire not less than 90 N/mm².
*   **Neutral Conductor:** 16 mm², XLPE insulated Aluminum, 7 strands, 4.4 mm compacted diameter, approx. 42 Kg/KMs mass, Max. DC Resistance at 20ºC of 1.91 Ohm/km.
*   **Messenger Wire:** 25 mm², Bare Aluminum Alloy (heat treated aluminum silicon containing 0.5% magnesium and approx. 0.5% silicon), 7 strands, 5.8 mm compacted diameter, approx. 65 Kgs/KMs mass, Max. DC Resistance at 20ºC of 1.380 Ohm/KM.
*   **Insulation Thickness:** 1.2 mm for 35mm² and 16mm² conductors.

**Technical Specifications for LT AB Cable, 2C x 50mm² + 1C x 25mm² + 35mm² messenger:**
*   **Cable Type:** LT Aerial Bunched Cable, XLPE insulated Aluminum.
*   **Core Configuration:** 2 Phase Conductors, 1 Neutral Conductor, 1 Messenger Wire.
*   **Phase Conductor:** 50 mm², Aluminum, 7 strands, 7.9 mm compacted diameter, approx. 127 Kg/KMs mass, Max. DC Resistance at 20ºC of 0.641 Ohm/km. Tensile strength of aluminum wire not less than 90 N/mm².
*   **Neutral Conductor:** 25 mm², XLPE insulated Aluminum, 7 strands, 5.5 mm compacted diameter, approx. 65 Kg/KMs mass, Max. DC Resistance at 20ºC of 1.20 Ohm/km.
*   **Messenger Wire:** 35 mm², Bare Aluminum Alloy (heat treated aluminum silicon containing 0.5% magnesium and approx. 0.5% silicon), 7 strands, 6.8 mm compacted diameter, approx. 95 Kgs/KMs mass, Max. DC Resistance at 20ºC of 0.986 Ohm/KM.
*   **Insulation Thickness:** 1.5 mm for 50mm² conductors, 1.2 mm for 25mm² conductors.

**General XLPE Insulation Properties:**
*   **Tensile Strength:** 12.5 N/mm² Min.
*   **Elongation at Break:** 200 % Min.
*   **Ageing in air oven:** Treatment at 135 ± 3ºC for 7 days, with max ± 25% variation in tensile strength and elongation.
*   **Hot Set:** Treatment at 200 ± 3ºC for 15 minutes under 20 N/cm² mechanical stress, with max 175% elongation under load and max 15% permanent elongation (set) after cooling.
*   **Shrinkage:** Treatment at 130 ± 3ºC for 1 hour, with max 4% shrinkage.
*   **Water absorption (Gravimetric):** Treatment at 85 ± 2ºC for 14 days, with max 1 mg/cm² water absorbed.
*   **Volume Resistivity of Insulation:** At 27ºC – 1 x 10^13 Ohm-cm Min; At 70°C – 1 x 10^11 Ohm-cm Min.
