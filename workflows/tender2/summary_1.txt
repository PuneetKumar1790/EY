### 6. Technical Specifications (Core Product Data)

This section summarizes every distinct technical specification from the tender, providing core product data for SKU mapping. The cables are LT Aerial Bunched Cables with XLPE insulation, rated for 1100 Volts. Applicable Standards include IS-14255/1995 (ABC cables), IS-8130/1984 (Conductors), IS-398/Pt.IV/1994 (Aluminium alloy conductor), IS-10418/1982 (Drums), and IS-7098(Part-II):85 (XLPE insulation). Phase and neutral conductors are Aluminum (IS 8130/1984), while the messenger wire is Bare Heat Treated Aluminum Alloy (IS 398/Pt.IV/1994). The insulation color is Black.

For the **3C x 35mm² + 1C x 16mm² + 25mm² messenger** cable:
*   **Phase Conductors (3C x 35mm²):** 7 strands, 6.8mm compacted diameter, approx. 95 Kg/KM mass, 0.868 Ohm/km Max DC Resistance.
*   **Neutral Conductor (1C x 16mm²):** 7 strands, 4.4mm compacted diameter, approx. 42 Kg/KM mass, 1.91 Ohm/km Max DC Resistance.
*   **Messenger Wire (25mm²):** 7 strands, 5.8mm compacted diameter, approx. 65 Kg/KM mass, 1.380 Ohm/KM Max DC Resistance.
*   **Insulation Thickness:** 1.2 mm for both phase and neutral.

For the **3C x 50mm² + 1C x 25mm² + 35mm² messenger** cable:
*   **Phase Conductors (3C x 50mm²):** 7 strands, 7.9mm compacted diameter, approx. 127 Kg/KM mass, 0.641 Ohm/km Max DC Resistance.
*   **Neutral Conductor (1C x 25mm²):** 7 strands, 5.5mm compacted diameter, approx. 65 Kg/KM mass, 1.20 Ohm/km Max DC Resistance.
*   **Messenger Wire (35mm²):** 7 strands, 6.8mm compacted diameter, approx. 95 Kg/KM mass, 0.986 Ohm/KM Max DC Resistance.
*   **Insulation Thickness:** 1.5 mm for both phase and neutral.

General XLPE insulation properties include a minimum Tensile Strength of 12.5 N/mm², minimum Elongation at break of 200%, and Volume Resistivity of 1 x 10^13 Ohm-cm Min at 27ºC and 1 x 10^11 Ohm-cm Min at 70ºC. The aluminum wire used in conductors must have a tensile strength of not less than 90 N/mm². Cables and drums must be marked with the purchaser's name (DGVCL/MGVCL/PGVCL/UGVCL), 1100 Volts, IS:14255/1995, Year of manufacture, and Trade Mark.
