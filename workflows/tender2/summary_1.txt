### 6. Technical Specifications (Core Product Data)

This section summarizes every distinct technical specification from the tender, providing core product data for SKU mapping and technical compliance checks. The cables are XLPE insulated Aluminum cables twisted over a central bare Aluminum Alloy messenger wire, rated for 1100 volts. Applicable Standards include IS-14255/1995 for ABC cables, IS-8130/1984 for conductors, IS-398/Pt.IV/1994 for aluminum alloy conductors, IS-10418/1982 for drums, and IS-7098(Part-II):85 for XLPE insulation. The Conductor_Material for phase and neutral conductors is Aluminum, while the messenger wire is bare heat-treated aluminum alloy (0.5% magnesium, 0.5% silicon). The Insulation_Type is XLPE, colored black, with phase conductors having ridges for identification.
For the **3C x 35mm² + 1C x 16mm² + 25mm² messenger** cable:
*   Phase Conductors: 35mm² (7 strands, 6.8mm diameter, 95 Kg/Km approx. mass, 0.868 Ohm/km max DC resistance, 1.2mm insulation thickness).
*   Neutral Conductor: 16mm² (7 strands, 4.4mm diameter, 42 Kg/Km approx. mass, 1.91 Ohm/km max DC resistance, 1.2mm insulation thickness).
*   Messenger Wire: 25mm² (7 strands, 5.8mm diameter, 65 Kg/Km approx. mass, 1.380 Ohm/km max DC resistance).
For the **3C x 50mm² + 1C x 25mm² + 35mm² messenger** cable:
*   Phase Conductors: 50mm² (7 strands, 7.9mm diameter, 127 Kg/Km approx. mass, 0.641 Ohm/km max DC resistance, 1.5mm insulation thickness).
*   Neutral Conductor: 25mm² (7 strands, 5.5mm diameter, 65 Kg/Km approx. mass, 1.20 Ohm/km max DC resistance, 1.2mm insulation thickness).
*   Messenger Wire: 35mm² (7 strands, 6.8mm diameter, 95 Kg/Km approx. mass, 0.986 Ohm/km max DC resistance).
Common technical parameters include: Tensile_Strength_min_N_mm² for aluminum wire in conductor is 90 N/mm², and for XLPE insulation is 12.5 N/mm². Elongation at break for insulation is 200% Min. Volume resistivity of insulation is 1 x 10^13 Ohm-cm Min at 27ºC and 1 x 10^11 Ohm-cm Min at 70°C.
