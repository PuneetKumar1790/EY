**2. Technical Specifications and Requirements**

The tender covers XLPE insulated Aluminum Aerial Bunched (AB) cables twisted over a central bare Aluminum Alloy messenger wire for L.T. Over-Head lines in Rural Electrification Systems.

*   **Cable Types & Sizes:**
    *   3C x 35mm² (Phase) + 1C x 16mm² (Neutral) + 25mm² (Messenger)
    *   3C x 50mm² (Phase) + 1C x 25mm² (Neutral) + 35mm² (Messenger)
*   **Rated Voltage:** 1100 Volts (Clause 2).
*   **System Suitability:** Three phase, 4-wire earthed system, working voltage up to 1100 V (Clause 4).
*   **Applicable Standards (Compliance Mandatory):**
    *   IS – 14255/1995: ABC cables 1100 volts (Clause 3(i)).
    *   IS – 8130/1984: Conductors for insulated cables (Clause 3(ii)).
    *   IS – 398/Pt.IV/1994: Aluminium alloy conductor (Clause 3(iii)).
    *   IS – 10418/1982: Drums for electric cables (Clause 3(iv)).
    *   IS – 7098(Part-II):85: XLPE Insulation (Clause 7).
*   **Conductor Specifications:**
    *   **Phase & Neutral Conductors:** Aluminum, XLPE insulated, compacted, conforming to IS-8130/1984 and IS-14255:1995 (Clause 5, Part A Sr. No. 11a).
        *   Tensile strength of aluminum wire: Not less than 90 N/mm² (Clause 5.3).
        *   Phase conductors to have one, two, and three 'ridges' for identification (Clause 5.2).
        *   Max DC Resistance at 20ºC: 35mm² (0.868 Ohm/KM), 50mm² (0.641 Ohm/KM) (Part A Sr. No. 12a, 12b).
    *   **Messenger Wire:** Bare, heat-treated aluminum alloy (0.5% magnesium, approx. 0.5% silicon), 7 strands, compacted, smooth round surface, conforming to IS: 398 (Part-IV):1979/1994 (Clause 4, Clause 6, Part A Sr. No. 11b).
        *   No joints allowed in any wire of the stranded messenger conductor, except those made in the base rod or wires before final drawing (Clause 6.2).
        *   Max DC Resistance at 20ºC: 25mm² (1.380 Ohm/KM), 35mm² (0.986 Ohm/KM) (Part A Sr. No. 12c, 12d).
*   **XLPE Insulation Specifications:**
    *   Applied by extrusion, fits closely, removable without damaging conductor (Clause 5.1).
    *   Color: Black (Part A Sr. No. 4).
    *   Thickness: Not less than 1.2 mm (up to 35mm²), not less than 1.5 mm (above 35mm²) (Clause 5.1, Part A Sr. No. 13a, 13b).
    *   Physical Properties (conforming to IS-7098(Part-II):85):
        *   Tensile Strength: 12.5 N/mm² Min (Clause 7, Part A Sr. No. 15).
        *   Elongation at break: 200 % Min (Clause 7, Part A Sr. No. 16).
        *   Ageing in air oven (135 ± 3ºC, 7 days): Tensile strength & Elongation variation ± 25% Max (Clause 7).
        *   Hot Set (200 ± 3ºC, 15 min, 20 N/cm²): Elongation under load 175% max, Permanent elongation 15% Max (Clause 7).
        *   Shrinkage (130 ± 3ºC, 1 hour): 4% Max (Clause 7).
        *   Water absorption (85 ± 2ºC, 14 days): 1 mg/cm² max (Clause 7).
        *   Volume Resistivity: 1 x 10^13 Ohm-cm Min at 27ºC; 1 x 10^11 Ohm-cm Min at 70ºC (Part A Sr. No. 14a, 14b).
        *   Carbon black content & dispersion (Clause 8(C)(vi)).
*   **Testing Requirements:**
    *   **Type Tests (Mandatory for submission of certificates):** Tensile, Wrapping, Conductor Resistance (for phase/street light conductors); Breaking load, Elongation, Resistance (for messenger); Physical tests for XLPE insulation (Tensile strength, Elongation, Ageing, Hot set, Shrinkage, Water absorption, Carbon black content & dispersion); Thickness of insulation; Insulation Resistance; High Voltage Test (Clause 8).
    *   **Acceptance Tests (Performed during inspection):** Tensile, Wrapping (phase/street light); Breaking load, Elongation (messenger); Conductor Resistance; Insulation thickness; Tensile strength & elongation at break; Hot set; Insulation Resistance; High voltage test (Clause 9).
*   **Packing & Marking:**
    *   Cables to be wound on **non-returnable wooden drums** conforming to IS-10418/1982 (latest version), duly painted (Clause 10.1, Part A Sr. No. 9).
    *   Drums must be robust to assure delivery free from damage and withstand handling/stringing (Clause 10.2).
    *   Drums must be suitable for wheel mounting (Clause 10.3).
    *   Each packing shall contain only one length of cable (Part A Sr. No. 8).
    *   **Drum Marking (Mandatory):** Manufacturer's name, Trade mark (if any), Drum number, Size of Conductor, Size of Messenger, Voltage grade, Number of lengths/pieces, Gross mass, Net mass, **ISI mark** (Clause 10.1, Part A Sr. No. 10).
    *   **Cable Embossing (Mandatory):** Purchaser (DGVCL/MGVCL/PGVCL/UGVCL), 1100 Volts, IS:14255/1995, Year of manufacture, Trade Mark (Part A Sr. No. 10).
*   **Length:**
    *   **Standard Length:** 500 meters with +5% tolerance (Clause 11, Part A Sr. No. 6).
    *   **Non-Standard Length:** Not less than 50% of the standard length, accepted up to 10% of the ordered quantity (Clause 11, Part A Sr. No. 7).
*   **Overall Tolerance:** Overall tolerance in supply of ordered total quantity shall be ± 2% (Part A Sr. No. 17).
