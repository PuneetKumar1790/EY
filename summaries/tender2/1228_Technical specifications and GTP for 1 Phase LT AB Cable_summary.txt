**2. Technical Specifications and Requirements**

*   **Product Description:** XLPE insulated Aluminum cable, twisted over a central bare Aluminum Alloy messenger wire, for L.T. Over-Head lines in Rural Electrification System.
*   **Rated Voltage:** 1100 Volts.
*   **Applicable Cable Sizes:**
    *   1C x 35mm² (Phase) + 1C x 16mm² (Neutral) + 25mm² (Messenger)
    *   2C x 50mm² (Phase) + 1C x 25mm² (Neutral) + 35mm² (Messenger)
*   **System Suitability:** Suitable for 1-phase - 2 wire & 2-phase - 3 wire earthed systems up to 1100 V.
*   **Conductors:**
    *   **Phase & Neutral Conductors:** XLPE insulated Aluminum, compacted, conforming to IS-8130/1984.
        *   Tensile Strength of Aluminum Wire: Not less than 90 N/mm².
        *   Identification: Phase conductors shall have one, two, or three 'ridges'.
        *   DC Resistance (Max. at 20°C): 16mm² (1.91 Ohm/km), 25mm² (1.20 Ohm/km), 35mm² (0.868 Ohm/km), 50mm² (0.641 Ohm/km).
    *   **Messenger Wire:** Bare, heat-treated Aluminum Alloy (0.5% magnesium, approx. 0.5% silicon), conforming to IS: 398 (Part-IV):1994 (latest amendment).
        *   Stranding: 7 strands, compacted for a smooth round surface.
        *   Joints: No joints in any wire of the stranded messenger conductor, except those made in the base rod or wires before final drawing.
        *   DC Resistance (Max. at 20°C): 25mm² (1.380 Ohm/KM), 35mm² (0.986 Ohm/KM).
*   **XLPE Insulation:**
    *   Applied by extrusion, fits closely, removable without damaging the conductor.
    *   Thickness: 1.2 mm (for 16mm², 25mm², 35mm² conductors), 1.5 mm (for 50mm² conductors).
    *   Colour: Black.
    *   Properties (confirming to IS-7098(Part-II):85):
        *   Tensile Strength: 12.5 N/mm² Min.
        *   Elongation at Break: 200 % Min.
        *   Ageing in air oven (135 ± 3ºC & 7 days): Tensile strength & Elongation variation ± 25% Max.
        *   Hot Set (200 ± 3ºC, 15 min, 20 N/cm²): Elongation under load 175 % max, Permanent elongation 15 % Max.
        *   Shrinkage (130 ± 3ºC, 1 hour): 4% Max.
        *   Water Absorption (Gravimetric) (85 ± 2ºC, 14 days): 1 mg/cm² max.
        *   Volume Resistivity: 1 x 10^13 Ohm-cm Min at 27ºC, 1 x 10^11 Ohm-cm Min at 70°C.
*   **Tests:**
    *   **Type Tests (for submission):** Tensile, Wrapping, Conductor Resistance (for conductors); Breaking load, Elongation, Resistance (for messenger); Physical tests for XLPE insulation (Tensile strength, Elongation at break, Ageing, Hot set, Shrinkage, Water absorption, Carbon black content & dispersion); Thickness of insulation, Insulation Resistance, High Voltage Test. An optional bending test on the completed cable is also specified.
    *   **Acceptance Tests:** Similar to type tests, including Tensile, Wrapping, Breaking load, Elongation, Conductor Resistance, Thickness of insulation, Tensile strength & elongation at break, Hot set, Insulation Resistance, High voltage test.
*   **Standard Length:** 500 meters per drum, with a +5% tolerance.
*   **Non-Standard Lengths:** Accepted up to 10% of the ordered quantity, provided each non-standard length is not less than 50% of the standard length. Each packing shall contain only one length.
*   **Packing & Marking:**
    *   Cables shall be wound on non-returnable wooden drums conforming to IS-10418/1982 (latest version), duly painted. Drums must be robust and suitable for wheel mounting.
    *   **Drum Markings:** Manufacturer’s name, Trade mark, Drum number, Size of Conductor, Size of Messenger, Voltage grade, Number of lengths/pieces, Gross mass, Net mass, ISI mark.
    *   **Cable Embossing:** Purchaser (DGVCL/MGVCL/PGVCL/UGVCL), 1100 Volts, IS:14255/1995, Year of manufacture, Trade Mark.
*   **Overall Tolerance:** An overall tolerance of ±2% is permissible on the total ordered quantity.
