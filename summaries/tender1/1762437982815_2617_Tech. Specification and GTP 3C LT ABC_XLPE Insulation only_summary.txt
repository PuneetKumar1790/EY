**2. Technical Specifications and Requirements**

The tender requires XLPE insulated Aluminum cable twisted over a central bare Aluminum Alloy messenger wire for L.T. Over-Head lines in Rural Electrification System.

*   **Cable Types:**
    *   3C x 35mm² + 1C x 16mm² (Neutral) + 25mm² (Messenger)
    *   3C x 50mm² + 1C x 25mm² (Neutral) + 35mm² (Messenger)
*   **Rated Voltage:** 1100 Volts.
*   **System Suitability:** Three-phase, 4-wire earthed system.
*   **Applicable Standards (Mandatory Compliance):**
    *   IS – 14255/1995: ABC cables 1100 volts (with latest amendment/revision).
    *   IS – 8130/1984: Conductors for insulated cables.
    *   IS – 398/Pt.IV/1994: Aluminium alloy conductor (and IS: 398 (Part-IV):1979 for messenger wire composition).
    *   IS – 10418/1982: Drums for electric cables (latest version).
    *   IS – 7098(Part-II):85: For XLPE insulation properties.
*   **Conductors:**
    *   **Phase & Neutral Conductors:** XLPE insulated Aluminum, applied by extrusion.
        *   Tensile strength of aluminum wire: Not less than 90 N/mm².
        *   Phase conductors to have one, two, & three ‘ridges’ for identification.
        *   Conductor properties (No. of Strands, Diameter, Approx. mass, Max. DC Resistance) must conform to Table-I for 16, 25, 35, 50 mm² sizes.
    *   **Messenger Wire:** Bare heat-treated aluminum alloy (0.5% magnesium, approx. 0.5% silicon), 7 strands, compacted, smooth round surface.
        *   Conforms to IS–398/Pt.IV/94.
        *   No joints in wire except those made in base rod or before final drawing.
        *   Messenger wire properties (No. of strands, Diameter, Approx. mass, Max. DC Resistance) must conform to Table-2 for 25, 35 mm² sizes.
*   **XLPE Insulation:**
    *   **Thickness:** Not less than 1.2 mm (up to 35mm²), not less than 1.5 mm (for above 35mm²).
    *   Must fit closely on conductor and be removable without damage.
    *   **Properties (conforming to IS-7098(Part-II):85):**
        *   Tensile Strength: 12.5 N/mm² Minimum.
        *   Elongation at break: 200 % Minimum.
        *   Ageing in air oven (135 ± 3ºC & 7 days): Tensile strength & Elongation variation ± 25% Max.
        *   Hot Set (200 ± 3ºC, 15 min, 20 N/cm²): Elongation under load 175 % max, Permanent elongation (set) after cooling 15 % Max.
        *   Shrinkage (130 ± 3ºC for 1 hour): 4% Max.
        *   Water absorption (Gravimetric) (85 ± 2ºC, 14 days): 1 mg. / cm² max.
        *   Volume resistivity: At 27ºC – 1 x 10^13 Ohm-cm. Min; At 70°C – 1 x 10^11 Ohm-cm. Min.
    *   **Colour:** Black.
    *   **Shape:** Compacted.
*   **Cable Lengths:**
    *   Standard length per drum: 500 metres (+ 5%).
    *   Non-standard lengths: Not less than 50% of standard length, accepted up to 10% of ordered quantity.
    *   Packing shall contain only one length per drum.
*   **Packing & Marking:**
    *   Wound in non-returnable wooden drums conforming to IS-10418/1982, duly painted.
    *   Drums must ensure delivery free from displacement and damage, withstand handling/stringing. Cable must be properly lugged.
    *   Drums suitable for wheel mounting.
    *   **Drum Markings (Mandatory):** Manufacturer's name, Trade mark (if any), Drum number, Size of Conductor, Size of Messenger, Voltage grade, Number of lengths/pieces of Cable in each drum, Gross mass of packing, Net mass of Cable, ISI mark.
    *   **Embossing on AB cable (Mandatory):** DGVCL, 1100 Volts, IS: 14255/1995, Year of manufacture, Trade Mark.
*   **Overall Tolerance:** +/- 2% on the supply of ordered total quantity.
